01Interactive Device

Tug-of-war

Express the tug and contradiction of human nature through a mechanical device controlled by brainwaves.

Hypocritical, selfish, ugly. In a corner without light there is endless self-pulling, contradiction, and self-blame. We dress up to show the world our brightest side, and in the private space that belongs only to us we keep collecting the things we cannot say.

Brainwave data from an EEG kit travels over Bluetooth to an Arduino system that drives eight reciprocating rods. The rods pull a transparent TPU sphere from every angle at once. Each pull is a desire, a regret, a numbness that reaches for something and never quite touches it.

Type
Personal project, 2023
Method
Mechanics, brainwaves, programming, sculpture, sewing
Tools
EEG kit, Arduino UNO, TouchDesigner, Bluetooth, reciprocating motors
Materials
Lithoplastic clay, LED light sphere, TPU film, metal frame
Heart rate is too simple. Myoelectricity is too easy to control. Brainwaves are disordered data, and that disorder is the closest thing to an honest inner self.

02User Experience Design

Zentouch

A user experience for relieving stress in the car, where a touch-sensing airbag lets the driver interact with the cabin in real time.

Work, housing, family. Young drivers carry a lot of pressure, and the car has quietly become one of the last private spaces they have. Zentouch answers that stress with touch instead of one more screen.

A soft, touch-sensitive airbag inflates when the system reads a low mood. As the driver presses it, the pressure data triggers sound, scent, temperature, light, and video, turning the cabin into a short restorative environment. Touching it feels like stroking a pet that is glad you are there.

Type
Personal project, 2023
Focus
In-car emotional AI, restorative environment, stress relief
Sensing
Facial expression (CNN / FACS), voice, pressure, biosensors
Build
Arduino UNO, Bare Conductive, air pump, graphene ink, TPU airbag

03Speculative Design

Plant Politicians

The first conference where humans and plants decide the future of the planet together.

We live where biogenetics, AI, and neuroscience are intertwined, and the home we depend on is quietly being rebuilt. Plant Politicians imagines the 1st Earth Protection Conference, June 5, 2073, the first time humans and plants sit at the same table to discuss the planet.

For plants to take part, they need a voice. PHC2K is a plant morphological language: deep-sensing cameras and AI learn how a plant's instantaneous form corresponds to its environment, so its receptors for water, heat, and light become legible as speech. The first sentence it forms is simple. Hello humans, I am a pothos, and I have something to say to you.

Type
Personal project, 2023
Premise
The 1st Earth Protection Conference, 2073. Attendees: humans and plants
System
PHC2K plant morphological language, PHC1K human information
Channels
Analog biosensors, deep-learning camera, chemical and electric signals, mycorrhizae
  • 1,000,000,000years since the first plant fossils
  • 200,000years of modern humans
